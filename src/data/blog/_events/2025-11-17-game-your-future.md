This weekend, SIG Members attened the [Game Your Future Conference](https://www.game4good.gg/game-your-future-conference/) at the Baltimmore Convention Center.

On Saturday, SIG founder and Emeritus Member [Richard Dansky](https://www.linkedin.com/in/richard-dansky-9b899a/) gave a talk and afterwards signed his book, [*The Video Game Writerâ€™s Guide to Surviving an Industry That Hates You*](https://www.routledge.com/The-Video-Game-Writers-Guide-to-Surviving-an-Industry-That-Hates-You/Dansky/p/book/9781032972619).

> **NOTE:** *You can win your own signed copy of the book, [and other prizes](/posts/arcjam-prizes-announced), at the [Game Writing SIG Arcjam](https://itch.io/jam/game-writing-sig-arcjam-2025) that begins this Friday.*

Later that Saturday afternoon, professional writers gathered for a coffee chat at Matriarch Coffee to talk shop and swap stories from the trenches. (Pictured above).
