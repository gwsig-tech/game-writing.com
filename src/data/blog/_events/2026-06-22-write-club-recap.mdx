On June 13th, we held our first-ever Write Club event at Narrascope. With over fifty  writers in attendance, we were completely blown away by the turnout. 

The night began with the formal presenation of the SIG's inaugural lifetime achievement award to the incredible Richard Dansky. 

![Richard Dansky](@/assets/images/events/richard-award-compressed.jpg) 

> For more information, [read the announcement at GDC](/posts/richard-dansky-lifetime-achievement).

Afterwards, we held our traditional Write Club writing contest, hosted by [C.J. Kershner](/posts/arcjam-spotlight-kershner). Four amazing winners were selected, much fun was had by all, and we'd like to sincerely thank every attendee for helping make this an unforgettable experience. 

![Write Club Winners](@/assets/images/events/winners2026-compressed.jpeg)

We'd also like to thank a few people in particular who's contributions helped make Write Club possible:

*  Allyson Gray
*  Alex Epstein
*  Tracy Steamster
*  Max Lincoln

We hope to see you all again next year!
