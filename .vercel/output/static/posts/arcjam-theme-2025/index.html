RESILIENCE: Game Writing SIG Arcjam

21 Nov, 2025
|

The wait is over! The theme for the Game Writing SIG ArcJam theme is:

RESILIENCE

For the next 72 hours, we invite you to explore this theme through interactive narrative. What does resilience mean to you? How do characters, communities, or systems bounce back from adversity? How can gameplay mechanics express persistence, adaptation, or recovery?

The interpretation is yours. Whether you craft a story about personal growth, survival against the odds, or the strength found in community, do you best to show us resilience in an interactive story.

Getting Started Right Now

If you haven’t already:

  1. Join the jam on itch.io - This is where you’ll submit your final game
  2. Join our Discord server - Get your Pro workspace upgrade and connect with other jammers
  3. Create your Arcweave account if you haven’t yet
  4. Use the Pro workspace upgrade link from the game-jam-announcements channel in our Discord

Jam Schedule

  • Start: Friday, November 21, 5:00 PM EST
  • Submission deadline: Monday, November 24, 5:00 PM EST
  • Community voting begins: Immediately after submission deadline
  • Judging period: November 24 - December 8
  • Winners announced: Monday, December 8

What You’re Creating

Remember, you’re not just making a game. We also encourage you to complete a portfolio piece. All shortlisted games receive personalized feedback from our expert judge panel on how well your game functions as a portfolio showcase.

Our community and the judges will evaluate your work on:

  • Storytelling
  • Gameplay
  • Theme Interpretation

You DONT’ need a team and you DON’T need audiovisual assets to make a game, submit, and win prizes. A solo, text-only game will focus your audience entirely on your writing and highlight your ability to craft an interactive story.

Prizes Await

Don’t forget that every submitted game earns a discount reward, and top games compete for amazing prizes including:

  • Professional development courses
  • Arcweave Pro workspaces
  • Career coaching sessions
  • Game writing textbooks
  • IGDA memberships
  • And more!

Support During the Jam

Throughout the weekend, we’ll be active in our Discord server to answer questions, provide encouragement, and facilitate connections between participants. Don’t hesitate to reach out if you need help!

  • Weekend Check-ins: Join us for an optional Discord check-ins in
  • Technical Support: Priority support available in the Arcweave Discord
  • Community: Discuss your progress in our Discord jam channels
  • Social Media: Drop pics and vids we can post, or link your posts for us to boost

Let’s Create Together

These are challenging times for game writers, but that’s exactly why we’re here—to remind ourselves and each other why we do this work. To experiment, to connect, to build something new.

For 72 hours, let’s explore RESILIENCE together.

The jam starts now. Show us what resilience means to you.

Questions? Join our Discord server or email info@game-writing.com. Good luck, and happy jamming!
