Arcjam 2025 Results: Celebrating RESILIENCE

10 Dec, 2025
|

After 72 hours of jamming, two weeks of community voting, and careful deliberation by our expert judge panel, we’re thrilled to announce the winners of the inaugural Game Writing SIG Arcjam!

The theme was RESILIENCE, and 123 teams rose to the challenge, creating interactive stories that explored personal growth, survival against the odds, and the strength found in community. Our judges wrote over 200 pieces of feedback, and the community cast 1,383 ratings on itch.io.

You can play the following winning games, as well as the remaining top ten games, on our 2025 Game Writing SIG Arcjam Page

The Winners

🥇 1st Place + Community Choice: Moments Away

Moments Away earned both first place overall and the Community Choice special recognition.

🥈 2nd Place + Excellence in Writing: Launch Day

Launch Day earned both second place overall and the Excellence in Writing special recognition. Both Judge Jon Myers and Judge Frankie Wakefield-Harrey gave this game a perfect score.

🥉 3rd Place + Judges’ Choice: Piranha Pit

Piranha Pit earned both third place and the Judges’ Choice distinction, along with a perfect score from Judge Wakefield-Harrey.

4th Place: Hold On, Valor

5th Place: I AM AN OFFICE PLANT & I PUSH BUTTONS
