Game Writing SIG Arcjam 2025: RESILIENCE

The inaugural Game Writing SIG Arcjam brought together 123 game writers and narrative designers for 72 hours of creative storytelling on the theme of RESILIENCE. Organized in partnership with Arcweave, this November 2025 event celebrated interactive narrative and built community during challenging times for the games industry.

By the Numbers

  • 72 hours of jamming
  • 123 submissions
  • 34 games shortlisted by 10 shortlisters
  • 200+ pieces of feedback from 6 judges
  • 1,383 community ratings on itch.io

Judging Criteria

Instructions: Give feedback like you would to an industry peer, not to a student or hobbyist. (1 = Mediocre - 5 = Outstanding)

  • Storytelling (1-5)
  • Gameplay (1-5)
  • Theme Interpretation (1-5)

AVE Judge rating (1-5) + Community AVE (1-5) = FINAL SCORE (2-10)
